Library Media Center

How We Help Students

The HEU Library Media Center is committed to supporting students in their academic and personal growth by providing access to a wide range of learning resources and services, both online and on-site. We offer various study spaces and computer facilities to create a comfortable and productive learning environment. Our librarians guide students in research, referencing, and information skills, while our support staff ensure that every student receives the assistance they need to make the most of the LMC's resources. Together, we strive to make the library a welcoming hub for learning, discovery, and academic success.
