How We Help Students

The Office of the Student Affairs is headed by the Office of the Student Affairs Officer (OSAO), who is responsible for monitoring the development, implementation and evaliation of all student activities, non-academic policies, and discipline process and special activities for international students and dormers in the campus Residence Hall.
